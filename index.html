Professional Summary

I have 15 years of professional experience: 8 years in Python, machine learning and data science; 4 years in IT infrastructure; and 3 years in business and innovation consulting. Currently working as a Postdoc Researcher at the Institute for Research in Biomedicine (IRB Barcelona), building Generative AI for generation of New Chemical Entities — dual target inhibitors — using latent diffusion, variational autoencoders, and transformer architectures on HPC clusters.

I have applied AI/ML across diverse domains: NLP, Computer Vision, drug discovery, biomedical imaging, time series, and process automation. I build agentic AI systems using LangChain, LangGraph, RAG pipelines (FAISS), and chatbots for WhatsApp, Telegram, and web. My tech stack centers on PyTorch, Docker, FastAPI, GCP, Ollama, and modern LLM APIs.

As an educator I have taught applied ML/AI, generative AI, automation (make.com), and Power BI to professionals in healthcare, freelancing, and industry. I hold a Marie Curie PhD fellowship and earned 3rd place out of 730 in the BBVA Data Science Challenge 2022.

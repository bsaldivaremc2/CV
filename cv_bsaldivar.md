### 1. Generation of Dual Target Inhibitors
Generative AI for drug discovery. Generates new molecules (text representation) that bind to 2 or more protein targets. Methods: latent diffusion, embeddings, contrastive learning, Python, PyTorch, cosine similarity, transformers, GPT-2, autoencoders, variational autoencoders, NLP.

### 2. Time Series Analysis for Breast Cancer Diagnosis
Evolutionary algorithms for feature engineering combined with XGBoost to predict breast cancer from multi-sensor time series. Keywords: Python, transformers, scikit-learn, repeated stratified n-fold cross validation, TPOT, MLJAR, AutoGluon, TabPFN, AutoML.

### 3. Company Growth Prediction Using Banking Data
Analyzed transactions, bank balance, and business features (sector, size, etc.) with AutoML frameworks and SHAP values to predict company growth.

### 4. SARS-CoV-2 Mutation Prediction
Applied CNN and MLP to predict recurrent mutations of the COVID-19 virus using RNA sequences, amino acids, positions, and RNA 2D structure. Used SHAP values and saliency maps for feature importance evaluation.

### 5. Heartbeat Detection from Infrared Camera Video
Applied feature engineering and random forest to detect heartbeat from single-channel infrared baby monitor video. Previous methods required at least RGB video.

### 6. "Which Scientist Looks More Similar to You?" App
Web app that detects faces in uploaded images, extracts embeddings with FaceNet, and retrieves the top-5 most similar scientists via cosine similarity. Tools: FaceNet, OpenCV.

### 7. Anemia Diagnosis with AI
After photographing with a phone, the conjunctiva (lower inner eyelid) is detected, cropped, segmented, and assessed for redness to predict anemia probability.

### 8. Microscopic Water Quality Evaluation
CNNs applied to classify microscopic biological samples to predict water quality.

### 9. Microscopic Saliva Evaluation for Pregnancy Detection
CNNs with transfer learning, Google Cloud Vertex AI, Cloud Run; deployed as an API for serving queries.

### 10. Patent Report Summarization, Clustering and Trend Prediction
NLP methods to generate state-of-the-art reports on coffee production technology for a cooperative company. Summarized, clustered, and drafted findings from thousands of patents.

### 11. Integration of Export Sectors with Patent Classification Categories
NLP to compare export sector definitions with WIPO patent categories, finding intersectoral relationships and predicting growth per sector/category.

### 12. Stomata Location of Coffee Leaves for Drought Resistance Prediction
Computer vision to count stomata on coffee leaves to predict drought resistance of coffee varieties.

### 13. VoIP Route Provider Optimization
Evolutionary genetic algorithms to find optimal call re-routing for a small VoIP company given call error probability (% dropped calls).
